**Cowbot Help**
Hi! Cowbot is a bot about cows (I mean, what more can I say?).
Cowbot is made by Nichodon, so yeah.

**Commands**
`//s <date>`:
        Displays Gunn Alternate Schedules
        `date`: Either a date in the format `m-d-y` or "`today`"

`//indent [c=<color>] <text>`:
        Echoes text in an embed
        `color`: Optional, is a six-digit hex color
        `text`: The text to echo in the embed

`//echo <text>`:
        Echoes text in a message
        `text`: The text to echo in the message

`//poll [num]`:
        Displays a yes or no poll through reactions
        `num`: If the text "`num`" is present, will display numbers

`//covfefe <person>`:
        Makes Trump tweet things about someone
        `person`: Whoever Trump does not like

**Banking**
Do `//bh` for help on the cowbank
